Mortgage Calculator






The purpose of this calculator is to help you determine how long it would take to pay off a mortgage and how much interest you would pay - given a specific monthly payment


Disclaimer

*This does not factor in fluctating interest rates and term lengths. As such the calculation cannot be exact.

How To Manually Calculate

Three primary variables:

Mortgage Balance, Monthly Interest Multiplier, Monthly Payment


The Monthly Interest Multiplier is determined:

(Interest Rate / 100) / 12 = Monthly Interest Multiplier


At the beginning of the month interest is added to the mortgage balance using the interest multiplier:

Mortgage Balance * Monthly Interest Multiplier = Mortgage Balance


At the end of the month the mortgage balance is reduced by the monthly payment:

Mortgage Balance - Monthly Payment = Mortgage Balance


This process repeats monthly until the mortgage balance is zero.
